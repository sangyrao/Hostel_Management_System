About hostel.

We are located in the historical heart of beautiful Bhubaneswar, near to all the touristic monuments, just a step from khandagiri Square. It makes this Hostel a great starting point for exploring treasures that Bhubaneswar has to offer. Forget about spending money on buses and taxis. Everything that you may care to see is within a walking distance from our place. Students staying here will be pleased to do so and will enjoy there each stay here.

  • The Institution provides facilities for hostel accommodation for both boys and girls. The hostels are spacious, safe and secured with a cordial environment and surrounded by pleasant landscapes.
  • A hygienic kitchen has been provided in each hostel with large dining halls.
  • The rooms are well furnished.
  • It has two on-campus girls' hostels accommodating one thousand students
  • hostels have round the clock Wi-Fi connectivity and medical facility.
  • Proper hygienic condition and strict supervision is maintained throughout.
